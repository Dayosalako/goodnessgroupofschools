About Us

Welcome to Goodness Schools, where excellence in education is our priority.

Head of School Introduction

Welcome to Goodness Group of Schools. Founded in 1995 by Pastor M.A. Adewale, our institution has been a beacon of academic excellence and moral integrity for decades. Located in Offa, Kwara State, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds, instilling strong values, and preparing students for a bright future.

At Goodness Group of Schools, we combine quality education, discipline, and innovation to shape the leaders of tomorrow. Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic learning ensures that every student receives the best foundation for success in life.

We invite parents, students, and educators to join us on this journey of excellence and transformation. Welcome to a school where knowledge meets character, and dreams turn into reality.
